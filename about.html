About DevSecOps

DevSecOps, a philosophy that integrates security practices within the DevOps process, aims to bridge the gap between development, operations, and security teams. By fostering a culture of collaboration and shared responsibility, DevSecOps ensures that security is considered at every stage of the software development lifecycle.
